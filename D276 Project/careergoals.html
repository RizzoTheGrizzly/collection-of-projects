Professional Goals

My primary career goal is to become a software engineer who creates reliable, accessible, and user-friendly applications. I intend to continue strengthening my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, version control, and professional software development practices.

As I gain experience, I would also like to explore user experience design and the relationship between technical development and human-centered design. I am especially interested in software that combines technical functionality with creativity, communication, and interactive experiences.

My long-term goal is to contribute to meaningful software projects while continuing to grow as a developer, designer, and a collaborative member of a professional technology team.
